Science

Science is used for researching new resources and improving how quickly you get resources. It is fundamental to progressing through the game.



Laboratory

Build a laboratory of your very own to double your Science per click.
Costs 20 Science, 20 Metal.



Unlock Resources

Unlocking allows you to gain different types resources to allow you to progress.

Oil

Oil is used for machines to gather resources. Unlocking Oil gives access to oil wells and fracking.
Costs 50 Science.



Gas

Gas is used for quickly processing Metal. Unlocking Gas gives access to land and ocean drilling.
Costs 100 Science.
