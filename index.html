Energy

Energy is created by power sources such as steam engines, solar power and burning oil.

Charcoal Engine: 0

Burns charcoal to produce a steady source of energy.
Each engine produces 1 energy per second.
Costs 50 Metal, 25 Gems.

Solar Panels: 0

Gains energy slowly from the sun without using any resources.
Each Solar Panel produces 1 energy per second.
Costs 30 Metal, 35 Gems.

Oil

Oil is used to build most machines that gather resources and also as a way of producing power.

Storage Upgrade

Upgrade your oil storage size to 100.
Costs 50 oil, 20 metal.

Small Pump: 0

Build a small pump to extract oil from the ground
Costs 60 Metal, 20 Gems.

Pumpjack: 0

Pumpjacks are much bigger than small pumps and produce oil on an industrial scale but they require a lot of energy. Each one produces 5 oil for 4 energy every second.
Costs 250 metal, 80 gems, 50 oil.

Metal

Metal is one of the primary resources. It is used for many things, including storage upgrades, machinery and most things in space.



Storage Upgrade

Upgrade your metal storage size to 100.
Costs 50 metal.

Miner: 0

Build a pickaxe for your miner.
Costs 10 Metal, 5 Wood.

Heavy Drill: 0

Heavy Drills mine metal at mass producing 8 metal per second. They use 2 energy per second.
Costs 160 metal, 60 gems, 50 oil

Gems

Gems are one of the primary resources. They are used for advanced machines and for powerful tools and components. They are more useful in later game.



Storage Upgrade

Upgrade your gem storage size to 100.
Costs 50 gems, 20 metal.

Gem Miner: 0

Build an improved pickaxe to mine gems.
Costs 15 metal, 10 gems.

Advanced Drill: 0

Advanced Drills mine gem at mass producing 4 gems per second. Because of the toughness of the drill needed it is slower than the heavy drill. They use 2 energy per second.
Costs 120 metal, 200 gems, 60 oil.

Charcoal

Charcoal is created by burning 2 wood.



Storage Upgrade

Upgrade your charcoal storage size to 100.
Costs 50 wood, 20 metal.

Woodburner: 0

Build an shovel for your woodburner.
Costs 10 Metal, 5 Wood.

Furnace: 0

Furnaces use energy to produce heat to burn the wood into charcoal. Because of the environment the process is more efficient and each one turns 5 wood into 3 charcoal for 3 energy every second.
Costs 80 metal, 40 wood, 100 oil.

Wood

Wood is one of the primary resources. It is used more often in early game for tools and buildings.



Storage Upgrade

Upgrade your wood storage size to 100.
Costs 50 wood, 20 metal.

Woodcutter: 0

Build an axe for your woodcutter.
Costs 10 Metal, 5 Wood.

Laser Cutter: 0

Laser Cutters slice trees quicker than axes and produce a lot more wood. Each one chops 6 wood for 4 energy per second.
Costs 50 metal, 90 gems, 40 oil.

Science Production

Science is used for researching new technologies to further your progress in the game.

Laboratory: 0

Build a laboratory of your very own to start producing science. Each one produces 0.1 science per second.
Costs 10 Wood, 15 Gems, 20 Metal.



Technologies

Research new technologies to unlock more mechanics and advance through the game.

Storage Upgrades

This will allow you to build storage upgrades to increase the maximum on the amount of resource you can have at once.
Costs 5 Science.



Basic Energy Production

You will be able to produce power from steam engines using charcoal made from wood in a furnace.
Costs 10 Science.



Oil Processing

Oil is used to fuel more advanced machines that gather resources and also to produce more power than basic means. Unlocking Oil Processing allows you to extract it from the ground.
Costs 15 Science.



Solar Panels

Solar Panels produce energy without the need for fuel, but they do it much slower than other forms of energy production.
Costs 15 Science.



Resource Machines

Resource Machines produce more resources than simple methods but require a constant supply of power to work.
Costs 20 Science.



Space

Unlocking Space allows for launching of rockets and opens a whole new field of research.
Costs 100 Science.

Rocket Fuel

Rocket fuel is created in chemical plants and is used to allow rockets to launch off into space.

Chemical Plant: 0

Chemical plants are used to make rocket fuel automatically. They require 100 oil and 100 charcoal to produce 1 rocket fuel. However, this is done slowly in 0.2 increments as the proccess takes 5 seconds for each plant to complete.
Costs 1000 Metal, 750 Gems, 500 Oil.
